I got pulled over. Sir, yes sir, sir, no sir... I didn't get a ticket. Generally I don't get good feedback on this video, many people think I got away with this unjustly. Unfortunately, I can clearly see this light was red, and I knew that when I went through it.
I included this video because it shows what training can do. This was a VERY tense moment. I had to make a correction in a split second. Stay off the front brakes, feather that back brakes, be ready to drop my tire into the dirt. It all happened with no time to think. Training worked. I instantly thought of, don't mash the brakes, feather rear only, hold fog line gently... it paid off. That decision was one of the fastest decision I have ever made. Could've been bad.
